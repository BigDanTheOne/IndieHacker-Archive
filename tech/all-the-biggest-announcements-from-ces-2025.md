# All the biggest announcements from CES 2025

**Author:** Katie Hignett
**Date:** January 7, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/rPGLfHb2XFcGsd9Dleu5

---

## Nvidia Highlights

Nvidia introduced "Project Digit," a personal supercomputer priced at $3,000 for developers and researchers. The company also unveiled Cosmos, described as "physics-aware" video generation models available on HuggingFace with permissive licensing. New GeForce RTX graphics cards were announced, including the RTX 5090 ($2,000), RTX 5070 Ti ($749), and RTX 5070 ($549).

## AMD Announcements

AMD revealed the Ryzen 9 9950X3D processor targeting creators, featuring 16 cores at 5.7GHz. The company also launched Ryzen AI 300 and AI Max series chips with "neural processing units" and new Radeon GPU options supporting FidelityFX Super Resolution 4.0.

## Sony's XR Tools

Sony previewed XYN, a spatial content creation suite converting regular video into 3D assets for extended reality headsets, including motion capture and photorealistic asset generation systems.

## Key Lessons

Powerful AI development tools are becoming more accessible to independent creators through both hardware and open-source model releases.
