# Hobbies aren't just fun - they're indie hackers' secret weapon

**Author:** Michal Kankowski
**Date:** November 30, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/t5fUICFUyKciKRwmoxOq

---

The article presents three research-backed reasons why hobbies benefit indie hackers:

**1. Hobbies Boost Confidence**

Research from Drexel University demonstrated that "just 45 minutes of creative activity -- be it doodling, molding clay, or collage -- can boost your confidence in tackling tasks." The author notes that confidence enables founders to take calculated risks leading to greater rewards. Creative pursuits in judgment-free environments naturally enhance self-belief.

**2. Enhanced Creative Thinking**

Activities like dancing, music, and chess sharpen cognitive abilities and increase creativity. A study in the Journal of Occupational and Organizational Psychology found that creative hobbies boost productivity. The author emphasizes that creativity serves as indie hackers' primary competitive advantage.

**3. Flow State Achievement**

Hobbies facilitate flow state -- that absorption where time disappears. Research indicates flow state increases productivity fivefold while enhancing creativity simultaneously.

**Key Lessons:**
- Balance prevents burnout while accelerating success
- Creative pursuits build confidence needed for entrepreneurial risk-taking
- Stepping back to play strengthens problem-solving abilities
- Structured rest enhances work performance
