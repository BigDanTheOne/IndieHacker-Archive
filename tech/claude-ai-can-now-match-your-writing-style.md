# Claude AI can now match your writing style

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** November 27, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/fE5iwjt7fBfBB7oCFraV

---

Claude AI now allows users to customize responses based on "communication preferences, tone, and structure." Three preset style options are available: Formal, Concise, and Explanatory. Users can additionally create custom styles to personalize their interactions.

**Key Lessons:**
- Style customization enables Claude to adapt responses to different contexts (professional communication, quick summaries, detailed explanations)
- Custom style creation allows users to align AI responses with their unique voice or brand identity
- This feature has implications for content creators, businesses, and students seeking personalized AI interactions

**Tech Stack Mentioned:**
- Claude AI (Anthropic)
