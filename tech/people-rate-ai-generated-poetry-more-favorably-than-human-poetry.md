# People rate AI-generated poetry more favorably than human poetry

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** December 4, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/X4LaatqhlZM0FQdHxYng

According to a recently published study, "AI-generated poetry is indistinguishable from human-written poetry." A Nature-published research study demonstrates AI can generate poetry comparable to human compositions. Survey respondents rated machine-generated verses favorably relative to human-authored work.

**Key Lessons:**
- AI can generate poetry comparable to human compositions
- Survey respondents rated machine-generated verses favorably relative to human-authored work
- The findings suggest AI poetry meets aesthetic standards previously associated with human creativity
