**Author:** James Fleischmann
**Date:** February 4, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/TpbhTVyp1sjBk0uzo4tR

## Revenue Numbers

- Starting point: $64K MRR (May 2023)
- Current: >$417K/month ($5M ARR)
- Pricing: $40/month entry tier up to enterprise plans
- Active subscriptions: 9,000+

## Full Article

Yasser Elsaid built Chatbase as an MVP allowing users to chat with PDF documents. His viral Twitter launch (to 16 followers) revealed massive demand. Recognizing PDF-focused tools faced commoditization, he pivoted toward action-capable AI agents for customer support, dramatically expanding market opportunity and technical complexity.

## Key Lessons

1. "Launch early and often" -- get products to users quickly for rapid feedback
2. "Listen to your customers" -- user input drives necessary feature development
3. "Focus on high-barrier opportunities" -- target spaces with low competition but significant potential
4. "Build in public" -- sharing your journey builds audience and community support

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: React, Next.js
- Backend: Supabase
- AI/ML: OpenAI's API, LangChain, Pinecone
- Original stack described as "lean, cost-effective"
