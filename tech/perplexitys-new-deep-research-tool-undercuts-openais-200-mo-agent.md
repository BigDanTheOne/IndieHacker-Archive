# Perplexity's new Deep Research tool undercuts OpenAI's $200/mo agent

**Author:** Channing Allen
**Date:** February 16, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/ZNM2yng7D5Z18YLVehLJ

---

Perplexity launched a "Deep Research" feature on February 14, joining Google and OpenAI in offering AI-powered research tools. The feature conducts multiple searches, analyzes hundreds of sources, and generates detailed reports. It's available free with daily query limits for regular users, while paid subscribers enjoy unlimited access.

On "Humanity's Last Exam" benchmark, OpenAI's Deep Research scored 26.6%, Perplexity achieved 21.1%, Gemini Thinking reached 6.2%, and GPT-4o scored 3.3%. Perplexity typically completes research in under three minutes, whereas OpenAI's version requires 5-30 minutes.

## Revenue Numbers

- OpenAI's pricing: $200/month

## Key Lessons

- Freemium models can democratize access to advanced research tools
- Speed and cost-effectiveness matter for bootstrapped founders
- Source attribution builds credibility in AI-generated content
