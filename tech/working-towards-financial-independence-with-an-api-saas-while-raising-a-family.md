# Working towards financial independence with an API SaaS while raising a family

**Author:** Katie Hignett
**Date:** August 6, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/l8gTzlBLNhN6I4NF4KsC

---

Dmytro Krasun, founder of ScreenshotOne, an API for generating website screenshots, is working toward financial independence while raising a family.

## Revenue Numbers

- Current MRR: $8,500
- Target: $10,000 MRR (expected within couple months)
- Product launched: Just over two years ago

## Key Lessons

1. **Ideal Customer Profile:** "Define your ideal customer profile and this will make your life so much easier"
2. **Persistence Over Comfort:** Founder considered quitting weekly but maintained determination through curiosity
3. **Strategic Gig Work:** Taking contract work to cover family expenses reduced financial stress and extended runway
4. **Product Hunt Impact:** Launch generated credibility, customers, and improved SEO (main acquisition channel)
5. **Realistic Freedom:** "Don't be delusional, you won't have complete freedom from anything"

**Tech Stack:** Next.js (dashboard), Astro (marketing website), Puppeteer (screenshot rendering), Go (API management), DigitalOcean (Kubernetes), Cloudflare (edge caching), Hetzner (GPU servers), Google Cloud Run
