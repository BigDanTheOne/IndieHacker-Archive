# Stability AI releases Stable Diffusion 3.5, their latest flagship AI image generator

**Author:** Stephen Flanders
**Date:** October 24, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/cUbYT4JP709fTHqK4Cok

---

Stability AI has released Stable Diffusion 3.5, positioning it as their flagship AI image generator upgrade. The company has faced significant challenges recently, including executive departures, financial constraints, and underwhelming reception to Stable Diffusion 3.

The new release includes three models: Stable Diffusion 3.5 Large (8 billion parameters for professional use), 3.5 Large Turbo (a faster distilled version generating images in four steps), and 3.5 Medium (2.5 billion parameters for consumer hardware, available October 29th).

Key improvements focus on realism, prompt adherence, and text rendering capabilities. The models aim to be highly customizable, optimized for consumer hardware, capable of generating diverse representation, and support multiple visual styles including 3D, photography, and painting.

Community response indicates the upgrade represents meaningful progress over Stable Diffusion 3, though Flux currently maintains a competitive edge.

## Key Lessons

- Iteration and responsiveness to market feedback matter when previous releases disappoint
- Accessibility across different hardware tiers broadens market reach
- Diversity and inclusivity in AI outputs are becoming standard expectations

**Tech Stack:** Stable Diffusion, Flux
