# Earned $1.2M, launched 5 startups in 2024 - indie hackers share their year-in-reviews

**Author:** Michal Kankowski
**Date:** January 1, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/g61Lu08M3otlLESYu9m9

---

The article profiles indie hackers' 2024 achievements and challenges.

## Revenue Numbers

- Marc Louvion: $1.2M
- Nico Jeannen: $350K
- Josh Mohrer: $450K MRR (Wave AI SaaS, from 200 to 22,000 paid subscriptions)
- Ankit: $200K total revenue (from $570/month)
- Idan Masas: $60K (age 20)
- JK Molina: $1.23M (2024); $630K (2023); $80K-$95K MRR

## Setbacks Discussed

- Jason Leow: Failed consulting and product launches
- Nico: Burnout, depression, and health deterioration from overwork
- Marc: Business-threatening technical issues

## Key Lessons

1. Timing and opportunity matter as much as hard work
2. Work-life balance prevents burnout and maintains long-term sustainability
3. Regular breaks and outside activities are essential
4. Community connection combats isolation in entrepreneurship
5. Success requires both grind and self-care
