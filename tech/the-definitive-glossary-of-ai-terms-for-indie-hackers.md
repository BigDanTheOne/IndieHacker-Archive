## Notable Terms Covered

The glossary includes foundational concepts (AGI, ML, Neural Networks) alongside emerging trends:

- **Vibe Coding:** "When a programmer describes a problem in a few sentences as a prompt to a large language model tuned for coding"
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP):** Open standard enabling LLMs to access external data sources
- **Chain of Thought (CoT):** Step-by-step reasoning methodology for enhanced transparency
- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG):** Hybrid approach combining data retrieval with content generation

The article functions as a reference resource rather than a case study or business narrative.
