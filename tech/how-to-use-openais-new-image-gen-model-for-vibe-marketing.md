# How to use OpenAI's new image gen model for vibe marketing - 10 example prompts

**Author:** Channing Allen
**Date:** March 26, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/CBy5LRJdRAUBbFKz0H3E

---

OpenAI recently released an advanced image generation model with significant improvements over previous versions. The standout feature is its newfound ability to render text accurately within images, addressing a longtime limitation in AI-generated visuals.

The article introduces "vibe marketing," a term for using generative AI images as growth marketing material. Channing Allen demonstrates this concept through ten practical prompts that can be adapted for startup branding:

1. **Quote cards** for social media with branded styling
2. **Comic strips** narrating brand stories
3. **Infographics** presenting data visually
4. **Landing page hero sections** with compelling headlines
5. **Product feature cards** highlighting key benefits
6. **Ebook/course covers** with professional design
7. **Event posters and webinar promotions**
8. **Call-to-action graphics** for conversion optimization
9. **Pricing comparison tables** formatted as images
10. **Lead magnet promotions** driving signup campaigns

Each example includes the complete prompt used to generate the image.

## Key Lessons

- "The most noteworthy of these changes, in my opinion, is the new text-rendering capability" suggests text accuracy fundamentally changes AI image viability for marketing
- Quality AI-generated visuals reduce dependency on freelance designers
- Systematic prompting produces professional-grade marketing assets quickly
- Vibe marketing represents an emerging standard for product-building workflows

## Tech Stack

- OpenAI's new image generation model (primary tool)
