# Report - AI to kill 92 million and create 170 million jobs

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** January 12, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/Jb0wwqdA71jgZur908u9

---

This report originates from the World Economic Forum's "Future of Jobs 2025" publication. The analysis projects that artificial intelligence will eliminate 92 million positions globally while simultaneously creating 170 million new roles, resulting in a net gain of 78 million jobs.

The piece emphasizes relevance for founders developing AI-powered software solutions. By understanding which sectors are experiencing the most significant disruption, entrepreneurs can strategically target their tools toward industries poised for growth.

## Key Lessons

- AI-driven disruption presents opportunity, not merely threat
- Job displacement will be offset by job creation at a ratio of approximately 1.85:1
- Strategic positioning in growing sectors offers competitive advantage for founders
- Continuous learning and adaptation are essential for workforce resilience
