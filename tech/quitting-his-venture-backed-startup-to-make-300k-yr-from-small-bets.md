# Quitting his venture-backed startup to make $300k/yr from small bets

**Author:** James Fleischmann
**Date:** September 1, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/powl5CXvHQRKROJSXpJ9

---

Louie Bacaj, founder of Motivation Stack LLC, shares his entrepreneurial journey. He left a prestigious position as one of the youngest senior directors at Walmart Global Tech, dissatisfied with the bureaucratic path to executive leadership despite high compensation.

After quitting corporate, he raised $1.5 million for a venture-backed startup but abandoned it after a year due to lack of customer traction and moral pressure to deliver investor returns. This experience prompted him to shift toward placing "small bets"â€”multiple independent projects rather than betting everything on one venture.

His first year pursuing small bets generated approximately $100,000 through diverse income streams: a 4-day online course ($50k), a short book, various tools, and other projects. By 2023, he earned $200,000, and in 2024, he exceeded $300,000 annually.

Bacaj emphasizes several practices:
- Time-boxing: Building within fixed windows (1-2 weeks) to avoid feature creep
- Customer acquisition planning: Identifying distribution channels before building
- Word-of-mouth leverage: Focusing on product quality to encourage organic sharing
- Boring tech stacks: Using familiar, simple tools rather than complex cloud infrastructure
- One-time payments: Preferring straightforward transactions over recurring SaaS models

**Revenue Numbers:**
- First year of small bets: ~$100,000
- 2023: $200,000
- 2024: $300,000+
- Monthly revenue: >$25,000
- Initial course revenue: $50,000
- Venture-backed startup raised: $1.5 million

**Key Lessons:**
- "One more feature will make it so everyone wants this thing" is a delusional trap
- Real estate diversification provided financial freedom to experiment
- Audience building on social platforms creates launch momentum
- Building in isolation prevents understanding actual customer problems
- Multiple smaller revenue streams provide psychological relief versus single high-stakes ventures
- AI development has democratized coding speed, but familiarity with your tech stack remains critical

**Tech Stack:**
ASP.NET with Razor pages, SQLite, VPS hosting, Claude (code generation), native iOS development, Gumroad
