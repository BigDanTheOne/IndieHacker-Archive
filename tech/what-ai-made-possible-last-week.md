# What AI made possible last week

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** October 20, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/TlR7JZYPlAZNZtIdK6IL

---

The article discusses three significant AI developments relevant to bootstrapped founders:

1. **AI Video Avatars:** HeyGen released version 3 with highly realistic avatars. Beyond Presence raised $3.1 million for similar technology. These tools enable non-video-comfortable founders to create content for platforms like YouTube and TikTok.

2. **YouTube's Dream Track:** YouTube expanded its AI audio generator, allowing creators to produce custom background music for short videos, simplifying video production workflows.

3. **On-Device AI Models:** Mistral released Ministral 3B and 8B models optimized for laptops and tablets, featuring 128,000 token context windows. These alternatives to major models offer privacy benefits and lower costs.

## Revenue Numbers

- Beyond Presence: $3.1 million funding round

## Key Lessons

- Video content accessibility through AI avatars removes creation barriers
- Affordable, privacy-respecting AI alternatives exist beyond major providers
- Combined AI tools enable faster product marketing
