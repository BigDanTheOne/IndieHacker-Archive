# TikTok hands out 10 tips on how to make long-form videos

**Author:** Stephen Flanders
**Date:** November 30, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/x5gIrQYA1A82sfrS7YTD

---

**Revenue Numbers Mentioned:**
- Long-form videos receive up to 5x more watch time than short-form content
- Creators focusing on high-quality long-form videos earned 15x more monthly than counterparts

**TikTok's 10 Tips:**
1. Structure content with clear beginning, middle, and end
2. Maintain consistent audience engagement throughout
3. Deploy strong hooks to capture attention
4. Use hooks that answer questions, tease results, or employ humor
5. Apply generous editing with location changes, trendy audio, and effects
6. Provide educational depth and teach viewers something novel
7. Solve viewer problems through myth-busting or practical life hacks
8. Emphasize production quality standards
9. Plan like a director, including costumes, lighting, and audio
10. Develop recognizable aesthetic consistency across videos

**Key Insight:** Long-form TikTok represents "the current asymmetric opportunity" for creators with editing skills, contrasting sharply with the lighter editing style preferred in short-form content.
