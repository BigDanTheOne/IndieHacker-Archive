# Four new AI models just got released

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** October 7, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/rSbvO4zreqjsAk5zgdEH

---

The piece discusses four recent AI model releases from Meta, Google, Flux, and Nvidia that present opportunities for indie developers.

## Gemini 1.5 Flash-8B

Now production-ready with a 50% price reduction and 2x higher rate limits compared to its predecessor. Pricing starts at $0.0375 per million input tokens. Google's product manager notes this model works best for "simple, higher volume tasks." Potential applications include real-time chatbots, news summarization services, and content classification.

## Flux

Released its API with pricing at 2.5 credits per image ($1 = 100 credits). The model excels at customization through fine-tuning, LoRAs, and ControlNet. Potential uses include creating virtual influencers and automated workflows.

## NVIDIA NVLM 1.0

An open-source multimodal language model rivaling GPT-4 in benchmarks. Its multi-modal capabilities enable applications like TikTok frame analysis tools and meme generators that combine images with text.

## Meta Movie Gen

Remains unreleased but offers impressive text-to-video capabilities with synchronized sound generation, enabling personalized video creation and video modification.

## Key Lessons

- Cost reduction drives accessibility for independent developers
- Open-source models enable self-hosted solutions
- Multi-modal capabilities expand creative application possibilities

**Tech Stack:** Gemini 1.5 Flash-8B, Flux, NVIDIA NVLM 1.0, Meta Movie Gen
