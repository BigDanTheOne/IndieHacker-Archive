# OpenAI wants to remove the AGI clause from its Microsoft investment

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** December 7, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/hv1kvhQTvppRfE5Qe2VV

OpenAI wants to remove the AGI clause from its Microsoft investment. The reason for this is to get a bigger investment from Microsoft. This decision suggests that OpenAI's strategy is shifting toward a more profit-oriented path.

**Key Lessons:** The strategic shift suggests prioritizing financial investment over governance protections.
