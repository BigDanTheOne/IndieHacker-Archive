# DHH joins Shopify's board of directors

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** November 20, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/zIgB2ppTjQMjZztLSE7q

---

The Ruby on Rails creator and Basecamp founder announced his new board position via his Hey platform. Shopify operates using Ruby on Rails and employs approximately one-third of the Rails Core Team. DHH expressed enthusiasm about furthering Shopify's mission of democratizing e-commerce access.

**Key Lessons:**
- Shopify's competitive advantage stems from leveling the playing field between small businesses and major e-commerce consolidated players
- Strong alignment between technology stack choices and organizational mission can create meaningful partnerships

**Tech Stack Mentioned:**
- Ruby on Rails (framework powering Shopify)
- Hey (DHH's communication platform)

**Notable Quote:**
"Leveling the commercial playing field between some obscure keyboard maker and the consolidated titans of e-commerce."
