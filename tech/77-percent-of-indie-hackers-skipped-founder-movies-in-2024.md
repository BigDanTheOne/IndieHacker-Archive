# 77% of indie hackers skipped founder movies in 2024

**Author:** Michal Kankowski
**Date:** January 9, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/WQoXtIx1KsVnZtj2mKrK

---

An X poll revealed that only 23.3% of indie hackers surveyed watched startup movies in the past year. The author contends that watching founder films provides cognitive and motivational benefits.

## Main Arguments

- **Neural coupling:** "When we hear a story, our brain syncs up" with characters, enabling emotional resonance
- **Underdog appeal:** These narratives trigger hope and inspiration lasting several days
- **Learning intent matters:** Watching purposefully (seeking insights) versus passively produces different retention outcomes

## Recommended Films by Category

**Overcoming Challenges:** The Pursuit of Happyness, Chef, The Donut King, Big Night, Fitzcarraldo

**Business Strategy:** Moneyball, Ford vs. Ferrari, The Aviator, The Corporation

**Hustle and Ambition:** Joy, The New Hustle, Indie Game: The Movie, Tetris, Jerry Maguire

**Series:** Silicon Valley, Undercover Billionaire, The Last Dance, Breaking Bad

## Community Suggestions

Startup.com, We Crashed, SuperPumped, The Playlist, The Founder, and Halt and Catch Fire
