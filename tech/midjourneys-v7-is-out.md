# Midjourney's V7 is out

**Author:** Channing Allen
**Date:** April 4, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/pm95OzapyuO3oFg8Zzmv

---

Midjourney released V7, its first new AI image model in nearly a year. The company announced this as an alpha-test phase with planned updates every one to two weeks over the next two months.

Key features include:
- Enhanced image quality with improved textures and accurate rendering of complex elements like hands
- Voice prompting capability for generating images through spoken commands
- Draft Mode for faster image generation
- Turbo and Relax modes offering different speed/quality tradeoffs

The release appears timed competitively with OpenAI's recent GPT-4o image generation upgrades. Community responses highlighted the reduced prompt complexity needed and improved natural aesthetics compared to previous versions.

## Key Lessons

- Simpler prompting requirements democratize tool accessibility
- Competitive pressure drives rapid AI model iterations
- Integration with workflow tools determines real-world adoption
