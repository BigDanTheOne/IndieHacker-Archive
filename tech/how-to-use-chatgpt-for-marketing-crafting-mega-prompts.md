# How to use ChatGPT for marketing: crafting mega-prompts

**Author:** Dan Kulkov
**Date:** September 26, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/sZbDPXoUeuTGAryBTcXO

An instructional guide with a six-step framework for leveraging AI in marketing strategy. The piece emphasizes practical prompt engineering and customization rather than generic AI usage, targeting solopreneurs managing limited time and budgets.

## Revenue Numbers

- Product pricing range: $69-$199
- Monthly earnings: $5,000
- Team size: 2 people

## Key Lessons

1. Establish clear context by defining ChatGPT's role as a virtual CMO with specific criteria for quality output
2. Provide detailed business descriptions including value proposition and target audience
3. Use systematic problem identification to prioritize marketing challenges
4. Break high-level solutions into actionable execution plans with concrete steps
5. Create checklists emphasizing often-overlooked practices rather than repetitive advice
6. Balance AI assistance with personal creativity and insights

**Tech Stack:** ChatGPT, FounderPal's Marketing Strategy Generator
