# Microsoft just discovered a new state of matter and then put it into a God chip.

**Author:** Channing Allen
**Date:** February 21, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/vzLeeaNRc7exXMqT2r3j

---

Microsoft announced the creation of Majorana 1, a quantum chip utilizing "topoconductors" -- an entirely new state of matter. After nearly two decades of research, the company developed this breakthrough using indium arsenide and aluminum layered with atomic precision, then cooled to near absolute zero under magnetic fields.

The article explains quantum computing fundamentals: regular computers use bits (0 or 1), while quantum computers use qubits that exist in superposition (0, 1, or both simultaneously). This allows quantum systems to explore multiple possibilities concurrently, theoretically solving complex problems faster than classical computers.

Current quantum computers remain impractical due to qubit instability and sensitivity to environmental interference. Topoconductors address this by creating topological superconductivity, which generates Majorana particles that protect quantum information more effectively than conventional materials.

Microsoft positions this breakthrough as transformational for quantum computing's practical applications, potentially enabling advances in drug discovery, materials science, and pollution remediation.

## Key Lessons

- Quantum computing requires architectural breakthroughs, not just incremental improvements
- Stability and scalability remain central challenges in quantum development
- Cross-disciplinary breakthroughs (physics + engineering) drive transformative technology
