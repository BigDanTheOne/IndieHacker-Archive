# The TikTok ban drops this week

**Author:** Michal Kankowski
**Date:** January 14, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/YPOiVFdTQv7AzNMV6diQ

---

The article outlines the impending TikTok ban in the US, set for January 19, 2025, unless ByteDance sells its US operations.

## The Ban Mechanics

The Protecting Americans From Foreign Adversary Controlled Applications Act (PAFACA) doesn't mandate deletion but restricts companies from updating, maintaining, or distributing the app. "It basically tries to choke TikTok off by making it harder to use over time."

## Current Status

Over 170 million Americans use TikTok. The Supreme Court was set to hear arguments January 17. Trump, who originally proposed the ban in 2020, now opposes it -- though Apple and Google could still delist the app regardless of political intervention.

## Revenue Numbers

- TikTok's US operations valued at $40-50 billion

## Alternative Platforms

Xiaohongshu (Red Note) and Lemon8 (ByteDance-owned) emerged as primary migration destinations.

## Key Lessons

1. Avoid over-reliance on single platforms; build owned channels (email lists)
2. Diversify digital presence across multiple social media outlets
3. Act proactively to preserve data before potential service disruptions
