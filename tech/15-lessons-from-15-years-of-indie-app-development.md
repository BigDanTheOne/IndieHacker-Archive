# 15 Lessons from 15 years of indie app development

**Author:** Lukas Petr
**Date:** December 13, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/PXLAwSt6ZCMCJzzJjlrK

---

## Key Lessons

1. **Enjoy the process** - Passion for craft sustains long-term effort
2. **Understand your motivation** - Clarify whether fulfillment or income drives you
3. **Risk vs. reward assessment** - Weigh lost corporate opportunities against autonomy and fulfillment
4. **Embrace unrealistic optimism** - Pursue goals despite unfavorable odds
5. **Success/failure distinction is thin** - Perseverance through difficult periods matters
6. **Find your niche** - Build products addressing specific needs you understand
7. **Provide sustainable value** - Create features competitors can't easily replicate
8. **Wear multiple hats** - Handle product, design, development, marketing, support
9. **Reframe self-doubt** - Ask "Am I doing my best?" instead of "Am I good enough?"
10. **Practice regular reflection** - Use weekly/monthly/daily reviews for improvement
11. **Know your strengths/weaknesses** - Lukas identifies strong finishing ability but struggles with marketing
12. **Apply lessons consistently** - Implement insights through small incremental steps
13. **Cultivate helpful connections** - Join communities of successful indie developers
14. **Acknowledge luck** - Success combines effort with fortunate circumstances
15. **Maintain perspective** - Balance enjoyment, business needs, and personal growth
