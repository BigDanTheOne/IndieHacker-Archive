# More and more companies are ditching the cloud. Here's why

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** November 7, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/UyfGWxXxK7thACQPle04

---

The article discusses the growing trend of companies moving away from cloud infrastructure back to on-premises or bare metal solutions.

Senior-level architects report a shift toward bare metal on-premises platforms over cloud infrastructure. Cloud storage and AI services represent particularly expensive cloud expenses. Companies benefit when they have personnel capable of managing bare metal servers.

Spiky traffic patterns make bare metal transitions complex; hybrid approaches may be prudent. Tools like Kamal facilitate containerized deployments for cloud exit planning. Compute costs significantly impact large organizations using AWS.

Companies referenced include 37 Signals and GEICO as examples of organizations making this transition.

**Tech Stack Mentioned:**
- AWS
- Docker (via Kamal deployment tool)
- Rails (mentioned in context of Kamal)
