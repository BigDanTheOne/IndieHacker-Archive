# Google open-sources DeepFold 3, an AI model that earned its creators the Nobel Prize

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** November 12, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/75vSBQQb3VBZ3fQgiT5n

---

Google DeepMind has open-sourced DeepFold 3, an AI model mostly used in biology and drug discovery. This came shortly after the model's creators, Demis Hassabis and John Jumper, were awarded the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for their work on protein structure prediction.

**Key Lessons:**
- DeepFold 3 has applications in medical/biology niches
- The model's creators received recognition through the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry
- Open-sourcing significant AI models can democratize access to breakthrough technology

**Tech Stack Mentioned:**
- DeepFold 3 (AI model for protein structure prediction)
- Google DeepMind platform
