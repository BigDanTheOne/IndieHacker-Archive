# Stripe is acquiring Bridge, a stablecoin platform, for $1.1 billion

**Author:** Channing Allen
**Date:** October 21, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/yPlJ46o8NyPOgD4hubUP

---

Stripe is acquiring Bridge, a stablecoin platform, for $1.1 billion -- marking Stripe's largest acquisition ever and signaling the company's optimism about cryptocurrency's future. Bridge provides an API enabling companies to issue and accept stablecoins.

Stripe CEO Patrick Collison explained his bullishness via social media, comparing stablecoins to "room-temperature superconductors for financial services," emphasizing how they'll deliver speed, coverage, and cost improvements globally. He declared Stripe's intention to build the world's leading stablecoin infrastructure.

One community member provided clarification on the superconductor analogy, explaining it represents frictionless, efficient transactions comparable to zero-resistance electricity transmission.

## Revenue Numbers

- Acquisition price: $1.1 billion

## Key Lessons

- Established payment processors are investing heavily in blockchain infrastructure
- Stablecoin adoption represents a significant technological shift in financial services
- Speed and cost efficiency drive enterprise interest in cryptocurrency solutions
