# Grok AI is now available on Telegram

**Author:** Channing Allen
**Date:** March 27, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/FdJqhsS4OPM5g8pyBZUs

---

Elon Musk confirmed that "Grok now on Telegram" on March 26, 2025. The AI chatbot is accessible to Telegram Premium subscribers at no additional cost.

## Why This Matters

The integration is significant because Telegram ranks among the most widely used messaging platforms within the indie hacker community. Additionally, users can access Grok through Telegram while avoiding the "distractions" inherent to the X platform.

## Access Instructions

1. Obtain Telegram Premium membership (Grok requires no extra fees)
2. Search for the official bot using handle @GrokAI within the app
3. Initiate conversation by selecting the bot and tapping "Start"
4. Engage by sending queries and receiving responses

## Key Lessons

- Cross-platform distribution expands AI tool accessibility
- Messaging apps serve as practical alternatives to social media for focused workflows

## Tech Stack

- Grok AI (xAI)
- Telegram platform
- Telegram Premium subscription requirement
