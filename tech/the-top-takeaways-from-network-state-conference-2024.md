# The top takeaways from Network State Conference 2024, one of the hottest events in tech

**Author:** Stephen Flanders
**Date:** September 23, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/X0LK9F38P00MvcSF9KXL

---

A 10-hour conference featuring 40 speakers addressing network states, parallel societies, and digital nomadism. The conference was organized by Balaji Srinivasan around his concept of internet communities inhabiting physical spaces while seeking diplomatic recognition.

## Revenue Numbers

- Digital nomads earn "$125,000 per year on average"
- Bryan Johnson has 2M+ followers
- Estimated 100-200 million digital nomads exist globally

## Key Lessons

**From Pieter Levels:**
- "The only other person on his team handles user data security" -- emphasizing security importance
- Choose locations based on personal preference, not tax policy
- Vanilla PHP and AI enable managing multiple businesses simultaneously
- Crypto payments via Stripe are returning after six years

**From Naval Ravikant:**
- Wealth represents "the byproduct of knowledge"
- Eight principles for effective tweeting: speak honestly, say it well, say something novel, write for yourself, make tweets standalone, eliminate extra words, use meaningful common words, remain opinionated while truthful

**From Bryan Johnson:**
- Modern brains face over-stimulation from food and social media

**Tech Stack:** Vanilla PHP, AI tools, Stripe (for crypto payments), Stablecoins
