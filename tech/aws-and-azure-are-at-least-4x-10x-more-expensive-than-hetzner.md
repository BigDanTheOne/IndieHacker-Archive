# AWS and Azure are at least 4x-10x more expensive than Hetzner

**Author:** Jeremy Theocharis
**Date:** October 25, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/LSMCfJsCoFsaCIL05B0o

---

A comprehensive comparison of cloud provider costs reveals dramatic price differences between major cloud platforms and alternatives like Hetzner.

## Cost Comparisons

- AWS On-Demand vs. Hetzner: 6.93x more expensive
- AWS 1-Year Reserved vs. Hetzner: 5.52x more expensive
- Azure vs. Hetzner: 10.14x more expensive
- UMH deployment cost: $32/month on Hetzner vs. hundreds on AWS/Azure

## Revenue Numbers / Cost Savings

- Ahrefs total revenue (2.5 years): ~$257 million
- Ahrefs AWS cost savings: ~$400 million over 2.5 years
- DHH/Hey cloud bill reduction (2024): From $3.2M/year to $1.3M/year
- DHH projected 5-year savings: Over $10 million

## Key Lessons

1. Cloud giants offer unnecessary complexity for many organizations
2. Vendor lock-in creates exit barriers
3. Self-hosting delivers superior cost efficiency
4. Industry leaders increasingly reject expensive cloud dependency
5. Small IT teams benefit from straightforward infrastructure solutions
