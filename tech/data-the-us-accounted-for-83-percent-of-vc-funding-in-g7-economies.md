# Data - The US accounted for 83% of VC funding in G7 economies over the past decade

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** December 4, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/nLpxS2g7wIdG1T8ozILv

---

A recent data report shows that most funding happens in the US, with 83% of VC funding in G7 economies concentrated in the US over the past decade. China is noted as a growing threat in R&D spending comparisons.

**Key Lessons:**
The piece highlights the dominance of American venture capital within developed economies and signals competitive pressure from China's rising R&D investments.
