# Meme coins outpaced AI in 2024

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** January 4, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/XY5sXp488QZZybfYPMo1

---

The GMCI Meme index delivered remarkable performance in 2024, achieving 320% returns while significantly outperforming other cryptocurrency sectors. AI-focused cryptocurrencies generated 91% returns, and the top 30 cryptocurrencies achieved 88% returns during the same period.

This performance shift reflects a broader market movement away from venture-backed cryptocurrency tokens toward community-driven alternatives. Platforms like Pump.fun have democratized token creation, enabling grassroots communities to launch and support projects directly.

The surge in community tokens corresponds with various high-profile scandals involving meme coins, indicating both the opportunities and risks inherent in this decentralized approach.

## Key Lessons

- Community-driven tokens are outperforming traditional venture-backed projects
- Retail investor participation is reshaping cryptocurrency market dynamics
- Accessibility platforms are fundamentally altering token creation and distribution
