# Chinese AI open-source models are outperforming their American counterparts

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** December 29, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/it35eqQvY1iytdkBAVGX

---

A new Chinese open-source model called DeepSeek-V3 has outperformed Llama 3.1 in several benchmarks.

For indie hackers, this matters because many SaaS products depend on open-source LLMs, particularly Llama. Awareness of newer, superior open-source models like DeepSeek-V3 could provide meaningful competitive advantages both short-term and long-term.

## DeepSeek-V3 Specifications

- 60 tokens per second (3x faster than V2)
- Enhanced capabilities
- API compatibility maintained
- Fully open-source models and papers available

## Key Lessons

- Competition in open-source AI is accelerating globally
- Performance benchmarks matter for SaaS product differentiation
- Staying informed about emerging models provides strategic advantages

## Tech Stack Mentioned

- DeepSeek-V3 (Chinese open-source model)
- Llama 3.1 (American counterpart)
