# Apple now allows you to apply to be featured on the App Store

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** November 15, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/QSVkjujlKcWrL3mGSvAl

---

Apple has introduced a feature called "Feature nominations." This is a way to get more exposure for your app. If Apple thinks you're "feature-worthy," they'll provide you with select placements on the Today tab.

**Key Lessons:**
Apple has launched a new nomination system allowing app developers to formally apply for featured placement on the App Store's Today tab. The company will evaluate submissions and select apps deemed worthy for prominent visibility.
