# AI startups are speedrunning to $100M ARR with barely any people on board

**Author:** Michal Kankowski
**Date:** February 20, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/qNIpbQUEz5cYymrkixsr

---

The piece contrasts pre-AI business scaling with current AI-driven growth. It notes that Slack required 2.5 years and 385 employees to reach $100M ARR, whereas modern AI companies achieve similar milestones with skeleton crews in months.

## Revenue Numbers

- Cursor: $100M ARR in 21 months (20 people)
- Eleven Labs: $100M ARR in 2 years (50 people)
- InVideo: $61M ARR in 16 months (80 people)
- Midjourney: $50M ARR in 1 year (11 people)
- Mercor: $50M ARR in 2 years (30 people)
- Cal AI: $21M ARR in 10 months (2 people)
- bolt.new: $20M ARR in 2 months (15 people)
- Lovable: $10M ARR in 2 months (15 people)
- MAGNIFIC: $10M ARR in 1 year (2 people)
- Aragon AI: $10M ARR in 2 years (9 people)
- SeoBotAi: $1M ARR in 9 months (1 person)

## Key Lessons

The article emphasizes that lean teams leveraging AI can achieve unicorn-level outcomes faster than traditional approaches. It suggests the next breakthrough will be a solo founder building a billion-dollar company.
