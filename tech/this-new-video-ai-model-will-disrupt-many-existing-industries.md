# This new video AI model will disrupt many existing industries

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** September 8, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/R361GJxWTP8QGPLycePs

---

A company called Infinity released version 2 of their AI model, capable of generating AI avatars with "eerily realistic expressions and emotion." The technology enables users to create videos within seconds by providing an image and text input.

Previous AI avatar tools like HeyGen and Synthesia produced "fake" and "robotic" results lacking emotional depth. Some competitors attempted addressing this by modifying pre-recorded videos' lip movements, but this approach creates problems when words don't match appropriate emotional expressions.

Infinity's solution generates expressive avatars that display contextually appropriate emotions. Current limitations include a 30-second maximum video length, though this restriction is expected to expand.

The article identifies four disruption opportunities: faceless YouTube content creation, short-form video advertising, virtual influencer content, and e-learning courses with engaging instructors.

## Key Lessons

- Emotional authenticity matters significantly in video content
- Current avatar technology limitations create market opportunities
- Realistic facial expressions enhance viewer engagement across multiple industries
