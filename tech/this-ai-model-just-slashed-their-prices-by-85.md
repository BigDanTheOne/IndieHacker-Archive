# This AI model just slashed their prices by 85

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** January 2, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/YCN8i4PPWRpNE95eiZmf

---

Alibaba has significantly reduced pricing for its large language models by nearly 85%. The featured model is Qwen-VL, described as "designed to perceive and understand both texts and images." The piece positions this development as advantageous for indie hackers seeking to create competitively priced AI products, noting that "Alibaba's LLM prices are now several times" lower than competitors.

## Key Lessons

- Pricing competition is intensifying in the AI model market
- Cost reduction creates market entry opportunities for indie developers
- Affordable LLM access enables broader participation in AI product development

## Tech Stack Mentioned

- Qwen-VL (Alibaba's multimodal language model)
