## Key Lessons

1. **Start charging immediately** - Real feedback comes when customers must swipe credit cards, not during hypothetical conversations
2. **Speed over planning** - Moving fast provides actionable data faster than lengthy planning cycles
3. **Presales strategy** - Generate revenue before product completion through forms and early commitments
4. **Community over audience** - Building deeply engaged customers matters more than vanity metrics like follower counts
5. **Leverage partnerships** - Integrations with complementary SaaS products create mutual value
6. **Time as currency** - Bootstrap resources efficiently by maximizing personal effort rather than paid advertising
7. **Competitive positioning** - Strategic messaging addressing competitor limitations resonates with frustrated users
