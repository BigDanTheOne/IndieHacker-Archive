# Short drama apps are a new, trending niche

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** December 25, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/pQ2v7ueNrdtYtjXTVES8

---

Short drama applications gained significant traction during 2024. According to AppFigures analytics, the sector's leading platforms include ReelShort (ranked 26th on the US App Store's top-grossing chart), Dramabox, and GooDshort.

## Revenue Numbers

- Average revenue per download: $19

## Key Lessons

The high monetization metrics -- specifically the $19 average revenue per download figure -- explain why new competitors continually enter the market. This indicates strong user demand for bite-sized dramatic content and demonstrates clear profitability potential for developers considering market entry.
