# Adobe launches a new video generator

**Author:** Darko Gjorgjievski
**Date:** October 15, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/nUj0TbxLBPz5OGLuzXE4

---

Adobe announced at its Adobe Max event the ability to generate videos from text using Firefly technology. The announcement included demonstrations of sample videos the model can produce. The capability integrates with Adobe's existing creative tools.

Community sentiment suggests prompt-based creation may eventually reduce reliance on traditional editing software, though the technology is still emerging.

## Key Lessons

- AI-generated content creation is becoming more accessible to creators
- Text-to-video generation could fundamentally transform media production workflows
- Rapid pace of AI advancement since ChatGPT's release continues to accelerate

**Tech Stack:** Adobe Firefly (video generator), Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premier Pro
