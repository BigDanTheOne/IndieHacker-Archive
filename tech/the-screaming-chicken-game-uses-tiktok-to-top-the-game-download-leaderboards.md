# The Screaming Chicken Game uses TikTok to top the game download leaderboards

**Author:** Stephen Flanders
**Date:** November 27, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/I5QnJacIiUcdqrmnjCHm

---

An indie game called "Mini Games" (better known as the "Screaming Chicken Game") achieved the world's most-downloaded game status by leveraging TikTok's viral ecosystem.

The core mechanic: players control a chicken through obstacle courses using vocal chicken sounds. This inherently shareable gameplay became a natural fit for short-form video content.

The article highlights TikTok's emergence as the premier platform for consumer app marketing, citing examples of young creators earning significant monthly revenue from apps launched via TikTok exposure.

## Revenue Numbers Mentioned
- 20 million downloads in October alone
- Referenced earnings from other TikTok-successful apps: $50k/month (porn-recovery app), $20k/month (life reset app)

## Key Lessons
1. **Virality by Design:** Successful products on TikTok need inherent shareability -- content users naturally want to create and distribute
2. **Organic Distribution:** Strong product-platform fit eliminates heavy marketing budgets; engagement becomes self-sustaining
3. **Simplicity Wins:** Complexity isn't necessary; straightforward, entertaining concepts outperform sophisticated incumbents
