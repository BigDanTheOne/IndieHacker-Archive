## Core Article Content

Daniyal Chughtai launched Facia to address identity fraud and deepfake detection after witnessing ride-sharing impersonation issues in South Asia. The platform offers facial recognition, real-time liveness detection, and deepfake prevention solutions primarily for fintech, healthcare, and government sectors.

**Product Suite:**
- Pay with Face (facial recognition payments)
- DeepFake Fraud Prevention
- Facial Recognition Ticketing
- Passwordless Authentication

## Key Lessons

1. **Charge Early:** "We started charging early, right after validating our MVP with pilot users."
2. **Pick a Niche:** "Start by focusing on a smaller, well-defined problem. But make sure it's a real, painful problem."
3. **Build Strategic Partnerships:** Regrets not prioritizing partnerships earlier; they accelerated scaling without massive ad spend.
4. **Balance Security & UX:** Early liveness detection felt "too rigid" and required countless iterations.
5. **Listen to Users:** "Solve one real problem really well, and keep listening to your users."
6. **Launch Early:** "Don't wait for a 'perfect' product — launch early, get feedback, and iterate fast."
