# Hitting a 7-Figure ARR by Taking on Deepfakes and Fraud

**Founder:** Daniyal Chughtai
**Product:** Facia
**Revenue:** $200K-$300K MRR ($2.5M-$3.5M ARR)
**Source:** [IndieHackers](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/bo6lqkKIDnPqqDEIhPhb)

## The Origin Story

Chughtai, with an economics/mathematics background and MSc in Big Data Science, previously worked in fintech fraud detection. The idea crystallized when a colleague's mother experienced impersonation on a rideshare app -- the driver wasn't the person she'd booked.

## Product Suite

- **Pay with Face:** Facial recognition payments eliminating PINs/passwords
- **DeepFake Fraud Prevention:** Real-time manipulated media detection
- **Facial Recognition Ticketing:** Venue access verification
- **Passwordless Authentication:** Secure biometric login

10 million+ end users across 200+ B2B clients.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

- Cold outreach and LinkedIn networking targeting fintech and government sectors
- Industry forum participation focusing on identity fraud pain points
- Word-of-mouth and case studies building trust organically
- SEO-optimized landing pages driving steady organic traffic
- Strategic partnerships with cybersecurity firms enabling scale without heavy ad spend
- Live product demonstrations building confidence rapidly

## Revenue Model

Usage-based B2B SaaS with tiered pricing. Startups, mid-size companies, and enterprises pay per facial authentication or API call. White-label solutions and custom integration services available.

"Start charging early." First paying customers arrived immediately after MVP validation.

## Key Lessons

**Pick a niche:** Focus on smaller, well-defined problems that represent genuine user pain points.

**Launch early:** Avoid perfectionism. Get minimum viable products to market quickly.

**Distribution matters:** Great technology requires clear audience pathways. Recommends reading "The Mom Test."

## Tech Stack

Python-centered for backend infrastructure, particularly machine learning and deep learning components.
