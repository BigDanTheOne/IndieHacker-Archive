# Everything I've learned from building and selling two companies

**Author:** Stephen Flanders
**Date:** August 1, 2024
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/starting-up/everything-i-ve-learned-from-building-and-selling-two-companies-cHdxrbKyDxbMSEG2bvOU

---

The article features an interview with Dhravya Shah, an 18-year-old entrepreneur who has successfully built and sold two startups. Shah shares nine key lessons for aspiring indie hackers:

## Time Management

Shah employs the "five-minute rule" -- completing quick tasks immediately and starting larger projects for at least five minutes to overcome initial resistance and enter a productive state.

## Idea Generation

Many ideas emerge from noticing everyday problems. Hackathons prove particularly valuable as structured idea-generation events, yielding successful projects like LMFAO.tech, Disco.pics, and Supermemory.

## Metrics Tracking

Shah regrets not collecting user metrics earlier. He identifies three growth drivers: virality (marketing funnel), retention (user engagement), and K-factor (each user bringing 4-5 additional users).

## Authentic Storytelling

Rather than criticizing competitors, successful growth comes from sharing genuine personal narratives. Authentic, enthusiastic posting builds genuine audiences.

## Product Hunt Strategy

While direct user acquisition may be modest, Product Hunt launches generate social media buzz and valuable feedback from 50-100 engaged users.

## Free Trial Model

Shah maintains free access until reaching 10,000 active users, preserving feedback channels while enabling monetization of 5% conversion rates afterward.

## Open Source Advantages

Open-source projects build trust, facilitate community growth, provide security benefits, and ease enterprise adoption.

## Developer-Focused Sales

Being your own user creates genuine enthusiasm that naturally drives network effects within developer communities.

## Core Philosophy

Success stems from building something personally meaningful, authentically sharing your journey, and maintaining genuine enthusiasm -- acquiring just ten committed supporters provides strong success prospects.
