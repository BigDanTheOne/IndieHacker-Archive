## Financial Philosophy

Feaster emphasizes: "Spend smart on the things that bring you happiness, without guilt." He prioritizes experiences over material possessions and advocates increasing income rather than extreme cost-cutting.

He maintains: "I'm chasing freedom over money."

**Key Investment Strategy:** Low-risk Vanguard ETFs, selective stock picks (bullish on Nvidia), no cryptocurrency interest.

**Debt Management:** Two mortgages only; refuses unnecessary consumer debt. Strategic about mortgage payoff timing given favorable interest rates (2.75%).

## Key Lessons Shared

- Save one year of living expenses before leaving employment
- Focus on experiences that inspire rather than material accumulation
- Play the long game; patience yields appreciation
- Increase income rather than pursuing extreme frugality
