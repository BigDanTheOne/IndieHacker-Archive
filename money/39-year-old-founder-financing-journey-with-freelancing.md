## About Sashe Vuchkov

Sashe Vuchkov is a 39-year-old founder building JSFreak.com (a Wordle-like game for JavaScript developers) while sustaining himself through freelance work.

## Financial Overview

**Income Sources:**
- Freelancing: $5,000 MRR (primary income)
- Founder salary: $1,000/month maximum
- JSFreak revenue: $0 MRR
- Emergency reserves: 2 years of expenses saved

**JSFreak.com Monthly Expenses:**
- Hosting/Cloud: $55
- Email/Tools: $41
- Design software: $20
- Development tools: $9
- Miscellaneous: $70

**Personal Monthly Budget:**
- Utilities: $317
- Food: $300-$400
- Leisure: $300-$400
- Taxes: $325
- Upwork fees: $220-$620

## Core Philosophy

Vuchkov emphasizes that "freelancing teaches you how to operate a business without requiring startup capital." He prioritizes skill development through reading and learning over traditional investing.

**Key insight:** He maintains that success requires creating genuine value and helping deserving people -- "the money will follow."
