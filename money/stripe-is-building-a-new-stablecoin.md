# Stripe is building a new stablecoin

**Author:** Channing Allen
**Date:** April 25, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/IvQnfk9CPqtpyHirU5oa

---

Stripe is developing its inaugural stablecoin product, leveraging technology acquired through its $1.1 billion purchase of Bridge, a stablecoin infrastructure company. Patrick Collison, Stripe's CEO, characterized stablecoins as "room-temperature superconductors for financial services" -- suggesting they enable breakthrough efficiency in moving money globally.

## Background on Stripe's Crypto Engagement

Stripe reintroduced cryptocurrency payments during 2024, enabling merchants to accept stablecoin transactions like USDC and Pax Dollar across Ethereum, Solana, and Polygon networks.

## Strategic Opportunities for Founders

The article identifies three pathways for developers to capitalize on this trend:

1. **Accept stablecoin payments** by integrating options like USDC or USDP at checkout
2. **Build cross-border solutions** using stablecoins for SaaS tools, marketplaces, and remittance services to reduce friction from slow transfers and foreign exchange expenses
3. **Explore programmable money models** enabling instant payouts, escrow arrangements, tipping mechanisms, subscription billing, and services operating independently from traditional banking infrastructure

The post frames this development as part of a broader industry shift toward normalizing cryptocurrency for business transactions.
