Snow Lee is the founder of PlugBear. He relocated his family from South Korea to San Francisco after securing pre-seed funding for his latest venture.

## Key Themes

Lee emphasizes that entrepreneurship provides the most efficient path toward building wealth from scratch. His philosophy centers on converting trust into financial opportunity. He advocates for investing in assets rather than liabilities, though he admits regret purchasing a $45k Tesla despite recognizing cars as depreciating assets.

His strategy involved building public visibility and leveraging existing networks to attract angel investors before finalizing product-market fit.
