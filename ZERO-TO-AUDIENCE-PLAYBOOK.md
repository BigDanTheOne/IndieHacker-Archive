# Zero to Audience: The Engineer's Playbook for Growing Followers Fast

> Synthesized from 552 IndieHackers posts across 7 sections, analyzed by 8 parallel research agents. Focused on **lean, time-efficient tactics** for technical founders starting from literally zero.

---

## The Core Truth (Before We Start)

Across 552 posts, one pattern is absolute: **you don't need a big audience to get traction.** Chatbase launched with 16 Twitter followers and hit $5M ARR. Sleek hit $10K MRR in 6 weeks from a single strategic tweet. Multiple founders with <500 followers got viral posts with 100K+ views.

The goal isn't "become an influencer." The goal is **build enough distribution to validate and sell your product.** Here's how to do it without becoming a full-time content creator.

## Part 1: X/Twitter — Still the #1 Platform for Indie Hackers

### Hack 1: The Viral Demo Tweet (Works With Zero Followers)

**The hack:** Record a 30-second demo of your product doing something impressive. Post it with a one-line hook. Don't tag anyone. Don't use hashtags. Let the algorithm work.

**Evidence:**
- **Chatbase** (Yasser Elsaid) — 16 followers. One tweet with a demo. Result: viral growth → **$64K MRR in 6 months**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/TpbhTVyp1sjBk0uzo4tR))*
- **Sleek** (Mattia Pomelli) — Strategic launch post with demo footage. Hit **$10K MRR in 6 weeks** entirely through organic X growth. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/pEvmU5qkWS6ny0AR9SUv))*
- **Tailscan** (Erwin Lengkeek) — 300 followers, posted a demo, got **70,000 impressions and 500 likes**. That's 233x engagement per follower. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/Lu8dn5Ged0vkcPostUe7))*

**Time: 30 minutes total.** Record screen, write tweet, post.

---

### Hack 2: Put Links in Replies, Not the Main Tweet

**The hack:** X deliberately throttles posts with external links. Move your link to the first reply instead.

**Evidence:**
- Elon Musk confirmed link throttling. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/2NSeAkN1dXjYliY5xMlT))*
- Indie hackers testing this saw **270% more views and 486% more likes** when links were in replies. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/q1W4ro1JImJEXIDlKC4q))*

**Time: 0 extra minutes.** Just change where you paste the link.

### Hack 3: Video Gets 10x More Reach Than Text

**The hack:** Switch from text posts to short video clips. X's algorithm heavily favors video content.

**Evidence:**
- **Rob Hallam** (SuperX) — Switched from text to video. **10x reach increase.** Grew from 900 to 40,000 followers. Hit **$23K MRR in 6 months**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/4d64o9ev4AXXhX9ogHQQ))*
- His content loop: (1) entertaining/vulnerable content for attention → (2) educational/product demos → (3) share results → (4) repeat.

**Time: 15-30 minutes per video.** Screen recordings count. No editing needed.

### Hack 4: Comment on Viral Posts in Your Niche

**The hack:** Find viral posts from bigger accounts in your space. Reply with something genuinely valuable (a demo, a design critique, a useful insight). The algorithm surfaces good replies to massive audiences.

**Evidence:**
- **AI game creators** with tiny audiences (300-3,300 followers) commented on Pieter Levels' viral game posts. Results: **1,500-2,900 likes per reply**, reaching tens of thousands. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/VKa1DsSICLniUWvWlsAg))*
- **Nick** (Baked Design) — Created 50+ design critiques responding to recent posts within 15-45 minutes. One post hit **110K+ impressions** → first client worth **$4,317/month** → scaled to **$1.2M ARR**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/RQaHrYUKBzkPoqnfMbHq))*

**Time: 15-45 minutes per reply.** Do 1-2 per day.

### Hack 5: "Comment for Access" Launch Posts

**The hack:** When launching, ask people to comment for early access. X's algorithm loves comments — it's weighted **13.5x higher than likes** for distribution.

**Evidence:**
- **Sleek** used this exact tactic. Crafted compelling post with demo footage, requested comments for early access. Massive initial traction. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/pEvmU5qkWS6ny0AR9SUv))*
- X algorithm ranks 1,500 posts based on interaction likelihood; **replies and profile clicks weighted 13.5x higher than likes**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/q1W4ro1JImJEXIDlKC4q))*

**Time: 30 minutes** to craft the launch post.

### Hack 6: Get X Premium

**The hack:** Pay for X Premium verification. It's described as **essential for growth potential** — verified accounts get algorithmic priority.

**Evidence:**
- Multiple indie hackers in the X algorithm analysis confirmed Premium as a prerequisite for serious reach. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/q1W4ro1JImJEXIDlKC4q))*

## Part 2: LinkedIn — The Underrated B2B Growth Machine

### Hack 7: Zero-Click Content (0 → 14K Followers in 18 Months)

**The hack:** Post content that doesn't require clicking away from LinkedIn. The algorithm penalizes external links (just like X). Share insights, frameworks, and stories directly in the post.

**Evidence:**
- **Adam Schoenfeld** — Grew LinkedIn from **0 to 14,000 followers** in under 18 months with zero-click B2B content. Got **8 pre-launch customers** organically with zero paid ads. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/starting-up/the-emerging-startup-playbook-sqDmfZOvr06ZpdMJqYb7))*
- **Joseph Lee** (Supademo) — LinkedIn thought leadership expanded reach from **10K to 500K+ quarterly impressions**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/U2wOWstNmRCIrAEAlMqm))*

**Time: 15-30 minutes per post.** Post daily.

### Hack 8: AI Content Collaborator (6M+ Impressions, Zero Ads)

**The hack:** Use Claude Projects to build a personal "content collaborator" — feed it your content pillars, brand guidelines, and top-performing examples. Write genuine insights, let AI amplify and format them.

**Evidence:**
- **Amos Bar-Joseph** — Built AI content collaborator "Shakespeare" using Claude Projects. Achieved **6M+ LinkedIn impressions** and **200+ customers** through organic founder content, **zero paid advertising**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/O3jTDG26L7DBGVewezUH))*

**Time: 10-15 minutes per post** after initial 1-hour setup.

**Evidence:**

## Part 3: Build in Public — The 3-Phase Strategy

### Hack 10: Phase 1 — Share Everything (0 to $10K MRR)

**The hack:** Share daily: what you're building, why, revenue ($0 is fine), decisions, mistakes, screenshots, code snippets. Transparency at this stage has zero downside and builds audience.

**Evidence:**
- **HabitKit** (Sebastian Rohl) — Built publicly on Twitter. Called it "by far the most successful way I've grown and marketed my apps." Reached **$15K/month**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/DCOYyF9O14dBnuGkkaQR))*
- **Oh Dear** (Mattias Geniar) — "Don't build in private. Be vocal on social media, even if it's scary." Result: **$1M ARR** through word-of-mouth. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/X876kKI7Olsf4YBI8FWb))*
- **Yoni Smolyar** — Posted daily vlogs for 450 days. Built **250,000 followers** before launching his app. Got **$26K in first 30 days**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/fYbp8x4WWqltdifUwCZJ))*

### Phase 2 — Ghost Mode ($10K+ MRR)

Delete revenue stats. Share non-metric wins: customer stories, product lessons, behind-the-scenes.

### Phase 3 — Selective Transparency ($30K+ MRR)

Share only occasional milestones. Annual recaps. Big launches.

*([Strategy breakdown](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/IhSJQBnXNuNwSuNTuz4t))*

**Time: 10-20 minutes per day** for Phase 1. You're documenting what you're already doing.

## Part 4: TikTok/Reels — The Level Playing Field

### Hack 12: Volume Beats Quality (6 Videos/Day Strategy)

**The hack:** TikTok gives new accounts equal algorithmic consideration as established ones. Post 4-6 short videos daily. Use format repetition — find what works, reproduce it systematically.

**Evidence:**
- **Viktor Seraleev** — 6 Reels/day (Spanish + English). Views exceeding **1.2 million**. Portfolio grew to **$60K+/month**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/myy3U7K9evxGOVOHti8s))*
- **Baked Bags** — 19 accounts, 2,721 Reels total → **1 billion views, nearly 1 million followers**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/3znZ4MnXFrhlr5CCVuio))*
- **Jenni AI** — "POV: You have an essay due" series → **300+ million views**, **$500K+ revenue**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/nGUpytBGMh0Y012hXpgh))*

**Time: 1-2 hours per day** for batching 4-6 videos. Use AI for scripts (ChatGPT → 10 scripts in 5 min). CapCut for quick editing.

---

### Hack 13: TikTok Long-Form = 15x More Revenue

**The hack:** TikTok is pushing long-form video. Creators focusing on high-quality long-form earn **15x more monthly** than short-form counterparts. Structure with clear beginning/middle/end, strong hooks, generous editing.

**Evidence:**
- TikTok's own data: Long-form videos receive up to **5x more watch time**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/x5gIrQYA1A82sfrS7YTD))*
- Hook strategies: answer questions, tease results, or employ humor.

**Time: 30-60 minutes per video.** Higher effort but dramatically higher returns.

## Part 6: YouTube — The Compounding Machine

### Hack 15: Three-Tiered YouTube Content Strategy

**The hack:** YouTube content compounds forever (unlike social posts that die in 24 hours). Use three content tiers: (1) evergreen tutorials, (2) clickbait with high conversion, (3) news-based trending videos.

**Evidence:**
- **Journalist AI** — This three-tiered approach drove **>$70K MRR** within a year. Key insight: **post 100+ videos before expecting meaningful traction**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/VPdRsMbHabCFfRXrzLPU))*
- **Magier** (Maximilian Fleitmann) — 3-5 videos weekly. "Longevity no other platform offers." **$150K MRR**. *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/vPFGPNTzzzsufHBQHmKa))*
- **Lane Wagner** (Boot.dev) — "Video builds trust" better than blogging. YouTube as primary growth channel → **$10M ARR**.

**Time: 2-4 hours per video.** Batch on weekends. Or use AI avatars (HeyGen, Beyond Presence) if camera-shy.

## Part 11: What NOT to Do (Time Wasters)

Based on multiple founder reports, these channels consistently **underperformed**:

- **Newsletter sponsorships** for audience growth (not revenue)
- **Referral programs** at early stage
- **Google/Meta ads** for audience building (use for validation only)
- **Twitter ads** for followers
- **PR outreach** and press releases
- **Cold emails** for audience building (fine for sales)
- **Startup events and conferences** — Pieter Levels: "Startup events are for losers...build in public instead." *([Original post](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/RKMqWylE52zbUN2UfdXN))*
- **Premature monetization** of community posts — OpenAlternative added $97 Stripe link after 250+ Reddit upvotes, caused immediate backlash

## The Daily Routine: 45 Minutes/Day

For an engineer with no audience and limited time, here's the minimum viable daily routine:

| Time | Action | Platform |
|------|--------|----------|
| **10 min** | Write one insight about what you built/learned today | X/Twitter |
| **5 min** | Cross-post to LinkedIn (reformat as zero-click) | LinkedIn |
| **5 min** | Cross-post to Bluesky | Bluesky |
| **15 min** | Record 1-2 short video clips (screen recordings) | TikTok/Reels |
| **10 min** | Reply to 2-3 viral posts in your niche with genuine value | X/Twitter |

**Weekly (2 hours):**
- Batch 5-7 days of content using AI collaborator
- Schedule via Typefully/Buffer
- Review what got engagement, double down on those formats

**Monthly (4 hours):**
- Create one lead magnet or free tool
- Do one Product Hunt / Reddit / HN launch push
- DM 10 potential partners/influencers

---

## The Priority Stack (For Engineers Starting From Zero)

1. **Week 1:** Set up X + LinkedIn + Bluesky + TikTok accounts. Get X Premium. Start posting daily demos/progress.
2. **Weeks 2-4:** Build in public daily. Comment on viral posts. Record short video demos.
3. **Month 2:** Create a lead magnet. Start collecting emails. Cross-post everywhere.
4. **Month 3:** DM 10 people with distribution. Propose specific, low-effort collaborations. Launch on PH/HN/Reddit.
5. **Month 4+:** Automate with AI agents. Double down on what works. Kill what doesn't.
