This IH+ subscriber-exclusive article provides an extensive glossary of AI terminology relevant to the indie hacker community. "Being an indie hacker in 2025 means you need to keep up with AI jargon."

## Key Terms Covered

The glossary includes 50+ terms organized alphabetically, spanning foundational concepts and emerging trends:

- **Foundational Concepts:** AI, AGI, Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Deep Learning
- **Practical Applications:** Large Language Models (LLMs), Chatbots, Computer Vision, NLP
- **Technical Processes:** Training, Inference, Fine-tuning, Tokenization, Embeddings
- **Emerging Trends:** "Vibe coding" (describing coding problems in sentences to LLMs), MCP (Model Context Protocol), Chain of Thought (CoT), RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
- **Hardware/Resources:** GPUs, TPUs, Compute

The glossary serves as a reference guide for both technical and non-technical indie hackers navigating the rapidly evolving AI landscape.
