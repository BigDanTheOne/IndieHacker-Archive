# Leaving an antiquated industry to grow an AI-native platform to a 7-figure ARR

**Author:** James Fleischmann
**Date:** October 15, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/tech/leaving-an-antiquated-industry-to-grow-an-ai-native-platform-to-a-7-figure-arr-T12Tq1C1AHG3QNFtzoJe

---

Joseph Lee transitioned from the food distribution sector to launch Supademo, an AI-native demo automation platform now generating mid-seven-figure annual recurring revenue. The platform helps companies create interactive product demonstrations for sales, onboarding, marketing, and training purposes.

## Origin Story

Lee's frustration while running previous ventures in perishables e-commerce inspired Supademo's creation. He struggled explaining products to non-technical audiences using traditional methods like slide decks and videos. These conventional approaches proved resource-intensive and difficult to maintain alongside rapid product changes.

## Growth Strategy

Supademo's expansion relied on four core pillars:

1. **Product craftsmanship** — maintaining simplicity while shipping weekly updates
2. **Product-led experimentation** — ungated experiences and free tools driving activation
3. **SEO and content** — programmatic tutorials targeting product searches; LinkedIn thought leadership expanding reach from 10,000 to over 500,000 quarterly impressions
4. **Product-led virality** — fully ungated platform access and reverse trials encouraging organic sharing

## Critical Insights

Lee emphasizes: "You'll learn faster by doing than by analyzing," advocating for rapid execution over extensive planning. He cautions against "confusing motion with progress," stressing focus on core metrics and genuine customer friction rather than activity volume.

Regarding founder-led distribution, Lee notes the importance of tailoring approaches to specific platforms and audience maturity levels rather than applying identical strategies everywhere.
