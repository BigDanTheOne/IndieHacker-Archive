The piece chronicles how the author's AI product studio launched BuilderKit, an AI boilerplate that generated $15,000 in revenue within three months.

## Product Strategy

The team differentiated themselves by including "15 pre-built apps" alongside standard boilerplate features, allowing users to "fork one of 15 pre-built apps, customize and deploy them within minutes."

## Marketing Channels

1. Twitter pre-orders and launch with demo video
2. Directory listings (100+) to boost domain rating to 21
3. Facebook LTD group launches
4. Product Hunt (#3 ranking, 600+ upvotes)
5. RocketHub marketplace partnership (70 sales)
6. Medium blog mentions

## Revenue Breakdown

- June: $3,147
- July: $5,527 (Product Hunt month)
- August: $5,261 (RocketHub sales)
- September: $865

## Future Plans

The founder intends to pursue SEO-focused blogs, YouTube video tutorials, free resources as lead magnets, and paid advertising exploration.
