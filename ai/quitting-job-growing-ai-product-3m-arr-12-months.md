Frank Sondors departed from his executive sales position to launch Salesforge, an AI-powered platform designed to help B2B enterprises integrate human effort with artificial intelligence for sales pipeline development. Within a year, the venture reached $3 million in annual recurring revenue.

## Core Business Strategy

The founder built Salesforge around his decade-plus expertise in sales leadership. He conducted 40 user interviews before development began and secured four paying customers prior to launching the product, validating demand early.

## Product Ecosystem

Sondors followed a "software compound model," similar to Rippling's approach, developing six interconnected products (Salesforge, AgentFrank, Infraforge, Mailforge, Warmforge, and Leadsforge) to expand customer value comprehensively.
