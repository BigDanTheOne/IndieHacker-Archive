# Making $20,000 MRR after only 163 days with an A.I tool

**Author:** AJ (interviewer); Dustin (founder featured)
**Date:** October 3, 2023
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/making-20-000-mrr-after-only-163-days-with-an-a-i-tool-5dec53c303

---

Dustin, the solo founder of Magai, shares his journey from pursuing acting in California to building a successful AI content creation suite. He describes moving through difficult entrepreneurial phases -- including a failed marketing agency, a scaled WordPress plugin venture (2014), and two unsuccessful product launches that cost over $65,000.

## The Breakthrough

His breakthrough came with ChatGPT's emergence. Recognizing limitations in existing AI writing tools that relied heavily on templates, Dustin identified a market opportunity. He built Magai in just 8 weeks using no-code tools, creating an interface that eliminated decision paralysis and barriers between users and their creative objectives.

## Growth Strategy

Dustin employed a "build in public" approach rather than aggressive early acquisition. His marketing channels include social media presence, affiliate partnerships, email marketing, and emerging paid advertising experiments.

## Business Model

Magai operates on a tiered subscription model ranging from $9 to $249 monthly, based on word limits and team member capacity. At the time of writing, the platform generated $200,000 MRR with 70-90% monthly growth, though facing a 20% churn rate. Operating costs were approximately $3,000 monthly, primarily driven by third-party AI model API usage.

## Core Advice

Dustin emphasizes understanding your audience deeply before launching, stating: "Never start a business unless you have spent a lot of time with an audience that matters to you."
