# Hitting $100M ARR in eight months with an AI software builder

**Author:** James Fleischmann
**Date:** August 6, 2025
**Source:** https://www.indiehackers.com/post/tech/hitting-100m-arr-in-eight-months-with-an-ai-software-builder-ONMaxjB3rix2PnBCnrDr

---

Anton Osika built an AI software builder that evolved from a weekend experiment called GPT-Engineer into Lovable, reaching $100 million in annual recurring revenue within eight months of launch.

## Background and Vision

Osika, with a physics background from CERN and experience at Swedish tech firms, identified a critical barrier: most people lack coding skills despite having entrepreneurial ideas. He created Lovable to democratize software development, enabling non-technical users to transform concepts into functional applications rapidly.

## Explosive Growth Metrics

- 2.3 million active users across 190+ countries
- 10+ million projects created
- 100,000+ new projects daily
- Monthly revenue exceeding $8.3 million

## Growth Drivers

The platform's expansion was fueled by community engagement and strategic decisions:

1. **Community-First Approach:** Word-of-mouth proved most effective, with a 6-week "Shipped" cohort attracting over 6,000 participants and a Demo Day offering $100,000 prizes.

2. **Social Media Presence:** Osika maintained active engagement across platforms, sharing customer successes and vision-related content.

3. **Strategic Moments:** A free weekend trial generated peak simultaneous usage of 134,000 users, demonstrating strong demand.

## Pricing Philosophy

The team embraced unconventional tactics, sacrificing $1.5 million in revenue by simplifying plans and making collaboration features free, prioritizing user activation over short-term returns.

## Operational Insights

- Team size remains approximately 40 people, emphasizing quality over quantity
- Swedish location provided access to talented developers from companies like Spotify and Stripe
- Faced significant scaling challenges, including a GitHub suspension affecting 500,000 users

## Key Leadership Principle

Osika emphasizes that assembling exceptional talent who believe in the mission fundamentally enables scaling achievements. Leadership involves recognizing when to delegate rather than micromanage.

## Future Vision

Lovable aims to expand significantly in the United States and become "the most important company ever created in Europe," enabling anyone to convert ideas into viable businesses within minutes.
